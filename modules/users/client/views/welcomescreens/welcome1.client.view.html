Voting and Commenting

The Hackathon App allows users to submit feedback on their peers' Projects and Ideas. Users can post comments and reply to comments from their peers! Additionally, users are able to vote on projects and sort the projects list by popularity.

Articles and Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Having trouble with a section in the app? The frequently asked questions section allows users to post and reply to questions asked by fellow Hackathon app users. Submitting a question is simple and responses are compiled for everybody to see. Need to post something important or visible to everybody? Submit an article through the home page.
